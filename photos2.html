Don't Belong


When you're ridiculed for being neurotic,
That's when you know you don't belong.
When you realise a pretty face is mere some evil in disguise,
That's when it's dawned upon, you don't belong.
When acumen is disgraced, when invudualism is looked down on;
When you defy competitions, since it's
Cruel to judge entirely different DNAs,
That's when they tell you, you don't belong.
When yielding to the abyss seems
More comforting than the blithe you get under the Northern lights,
That's when you realise, you don't belong.
